How to Grow a Healthy Peace Lily Plant

Temperature

15°C to 26°C

Water

1 times/week

Planting Steps

  1. Choose a pot with good drainage.
  2. Fill with a peat-based potting mix.
  3. Gently place the Peace Lily root ball in the center.
  4. Water lightly and place in indirect light.

1. Choose the Right Peace Lily Type

  • Common varieties: Mauna Loa, Domino, Sensation, Little Angel.
  • Choose based on your space and light conditions.
  • Type: Light, well-aerated potting soil with peat moss.
  • pH Level: Slightly acidic (5.8–6.5).
  • Preparation: Add perlite or coarse sand to improve drainage.
  • Water once the top inch of soil feels dry.
  • Peace Lilies prefer consistently moist (not soggy) soil.
  • Mist leaves in dry indoor environments.
  • Organic Options: Fish emulsion, compost tea, seaweed extract.
  • Chemical Fertilizers: Diluted houseplant fertilizer (20-20-20).
  • Fertilize once a month during spring and summer.
  • Bright, indirect light is ideal.
  • Avoid direct sunlight which can scorch leaves.
  • Suitable for indoor spaces like offices and bedrooms.

Final Tips

Wipe leaves with a damp cloth to keep them dust-free. Peace Lilies help purify indoor air. Repot every 1–2 years for healthy growth.

Peace Lily Plant Care Tips

  • Pruning: Remove yellow or brown leaves to encourage new growth.
  • Pest Control: Watch for mealybugs or spider mites; use neem oil if needed.
  • Disease Prevention: Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot and leaf blight.
